Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. What We Collect

During a session, Ojaq processes:

  • Voice audio — streamed in real-time to Google's Gemini AI for conversation. We do not store audio recordings.
  • Transcripts — generated by Gemini during the session. Used for real-time presence analysis only. We do not store conversation transcripts on our servers.
  • Presence data — anonymized emotional presence scores (energy, confidence, resistance, engagement, congruence, sentiment). We store these scores without any identifying information to improve the service. No words or conversation content is included.
  • Email address — if you join the waitlist. Used only to notify you about Ojaq.

2. What We Don't Collect

  • We do not store your voice recordings.
  • We do not store conversation transcripts on our servers.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use your conversations to train AI models.

3. Third-Party Services

Ojaq uses Google's Gemini AI for voice conversation and presence analysis. Your voice and text data are sent to Google's servers during a session. Google's Gemini API Terms apply to that processing.

4. Local Storage

The Ojaq playground may store settings and session preferences in your browser's local storage. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

5. Data Security

All connections use HTTPS/WSS encryption. Voice data is streamed, not stored. We implement reasonable security measures to protect the service.

6. Your Rights

We store only anonymized presence scores (no words, no audio, no transcripts). If you joined the waitlist and want your email removed, contact us.

7. Children

Ojaq is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the "last updated" date above.
