What this is

Most conversations happen on the surface. Ojaq goes underneath.

You speak. Ojaq listens — not just to your words, but to what's alive beneath them. The energy. The hesitation. The thing you almost said but didn't.

When it speaks back, it's rarely and precisely. One sentence. Two at most. Only reflections so accurate they feel uncanny.

Session frameworks

Different lenses on the same intelligence.

C

Coaching

Asks the question you haven't asked yourself. Finds the smallest next step. Challenges gently when you're avoiding.

S

Self-Discovery

A mirror. Never asks. Never advises. Only names what it observes. "There's something careful about how you said that."

F

Friend

Someone who actually listens. Can joke. Can call you out. Gets quiet when something's heavy. Just real.

T

Together — For two

A third voice in the room. For two people who want a witness, not a mediator. Hears what moves between them. Speaks rarely. Listens fully.
